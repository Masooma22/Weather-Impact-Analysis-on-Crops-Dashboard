About the project

Bridging weather data
with agricultural outcomes

Pakistan's agricultural sector faces growing threats from climate change. AgroInsight was designed to bridge the critical gap between raw meteorological data and real farming decisions — giving every stakeholder the intelligence they need, when they need it.

Real-time environmental monitoring

Live temperature, humidity, rainfall and wind data for any district across Punjab and beyond.

Machine learning crop forecasting

10 to 30 day predictions for wheat, rice, cotton, and other key crops trained on historical datasets.

Instant disaster early warnings

Flood, drought, and heatwave alerts delivered in under 10 seconds with actionable safety protocols.

Economic impact visualization

Interactive dashboards linking weather patterns to market prices and farmer income trends.
