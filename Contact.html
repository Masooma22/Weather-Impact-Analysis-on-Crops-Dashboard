FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is AgroInsight free to use?
Yes — AgroInsight is a final year research project developed at UAF Faisalabad. The platform is currently free for all registered users including farmers, students, and researchers.
Which crops are supported?
Currently over 10 crops are supported including wheat, rice, cotton, sugarcane, maize, and others. We plan to expand coverage with each development increment.
How accurate are the AI predictions?
Our machine learning models achieve 80%+ precision on short-term (10–30 day) crop yield forecasts, trained on historical Punjab weather and agricultural data.
How fast are disaster alerts delivered?
Disaster alerts are delivered in under 10 seconds via the dashboard and optionally via SMS, giving farmers critical time to act before extreme weather events strike.
Can I download weather reports?
Yes. The platform allows users to generate and download weather impact reports as PDFs for offline reference, sharing with other farmers, or presenting to stakeholders.
How can I collaborate or contribute data?
We welcome contributions from researchers, agricultural organizations, and developers. Use the contact form above or reach out directly to the project team at UAF.
